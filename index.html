TWEE

TWEE is a research-based music project led by Malou and Xiao-wan started in 2023.

The goal is to craft deep, textured soundscapes that blend elements of the past and the present, providing a material and auditory presence that invites ongoing interpretation and reflection.

Keywords: spatial audio, neural audio synthesis, improvisation
